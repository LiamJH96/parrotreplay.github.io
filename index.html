Frequently Asked Questions

Can I transfer my license to a new computer?

+

Licenses are hardware-locked to your specific computer. If you upgrade your hardware significantly (e.g., motherboard replacement), contact us for a replacement license. We grant these at our discretion.

Does Parrot Replay require internet connection?

+

No! Parrot Replay is 100% offline. No telemetry, no cloud storage, no internet required. Your audio recordings stay completely private on your machine.

What's the difference between Standard and PRO?

+

Standard includes 3 profiles with all core features. PRO unlocks 6 additional profiles (9 total) and adds Import/Export functionality for backing up and sharing your clip collections.

Will this get me banned from games?

+

Parrot Replay is an audio tool, not a game cheat. However, you are responsible for following game rules and platform terms of service. Use responsibly and check your game's specific policies.

Can I use this with Discord/OBS/Streaming?

+

Yes! Set your Discord/OBS input to VB-Audio Virtual Cable, and Parrot Replay will route audio there. You can also use local playback mode to preview clips privately.

What if I don't have a NumPad on my keyboard?

+

Parrot Replay includes a full hotkey customization system! Rebind all controls to any keys you want using the visual hotkey editor with live key capture.
